Instructions

  1. Ensure RAK4631 is connected and powered
  2. Upload a Nordic DFU package (.zip file)
  3. Click "Enter DFU Mode" on the dashboard to prepare the RAK module
  4. Click "Start Flashing" to begin the firmware update
  5. Wait for the process to complete (typically 1-3 minutes)
  6. The RAK module will reboot automatically when complete
Tip: If flashing fails, try entering DFU mode manually by double-tapping the RAK reset button within 500ms.
