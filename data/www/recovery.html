Recovery Instructions

  1. Connect SWD pins (SWCLK and SWDIO) between ESP32 and RAK4631
  2. Ensure both devices share a common ground connection
  3. Click "Test Connection" to verify SWD communication
  4. Backup first! Click "Backup Firmware" to save a full flash image
  5. If recovering: Mass erase, then flash your backup or a fresh bootloader
  6. Once bootloader is flashed, use normal firmware update method
Note: After recovery, you'll need to flash both the bootloader and application firmware.
